About Our Past Stories

1960s

1963

Ngee Ann College was inaugurated on May 25, 1963. The College offered four-year degree courses in three main areas: language, commerce and technology.

Learn More

1967

The College became a legal independent institution governed by a Council, and started offering engineering and commerce courses at diploma level.

Learn More

1968

The College changed its name to Ngee Ann Technical College. The College moved from the Teochew Building at Tank Road to its Clementi campus.
